Z-type Foldable Container House is the most popular type of box specially used for export. Compared with traditional container house, the installation speed is 90% faster. The electrical houses circuits, doors and windows are all assembled in advance, and only the screws need to be installed when they arrive.

Z-type Foldable Container Houses are also called mobile folding containers because they are movable and can be disassembled and reassembled. There are various devices inside that can be folded up and are so convenient to use that more and more people are using them now.

Z-type Foldable Packing Container – Shipping Version

The Z-type foldable container is optimized for sea freight. It comes in two configurations based on wall panel thickness and loading efficiency:

Option 1: 50mm Wall Panel

  • Expanded Size: 5990mm × 2500mm × 2470mm
  • Folded Size: 5990mm × 2500mm × 440mm
  • Weight: 1.26 tons
  • Loading Capacity: 10 units per 40ft high cube container

Option 2: 60mm Wall Panel

  • Expanded Size: 5990mm × 2500mm × 2470mm
  • Folded Size: 5900mm × 2500mm × 510mm
  • Weight: 1.35 tons
  • Loading Capacity: 8 units per 40ft high cube container
